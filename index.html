Andrew Serrano, Alondra Torres & Tamanna Zahir

Date: 12 May, 2024

Elevator Pitch

Our goal is to create a website to help CSUMB students who are struggling with food insecurity (50.1%) and housing insecurity (15.9%). Our project is targeted at students from low socioeconomic backgrounds or those facing hard times during their education at CSUMB.

Objective

We aim to assist students in overcoming the challenges of securing basic needs, thereby enhancing their academic success. The website serves as a one-stop resource connecting them with necessary services for food, housing, healthcare, and financial assistance.

Implementation

The website features sections on affordable housing, healthcare, EBT/food banks, EOPS for textbooks, and financial aid/scholarships. It includes detailed guides on how to access these resources, ensuring that students can easily navigate through the available support.

Accessibility

This resource is designed to be accessible throughout a student's tenure at CSUMB and potentially beyond, easing the burden of daily survival to focus more on their studies.
