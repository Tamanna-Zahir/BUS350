Housing Insecurity

CSUMB Housing Availability

Housing is available to full time students enrolled at CSUMB. Full time freshman and sophomore students that do not live in the tri county area are required to live on campus.

Steps for Applying

  1. Sign into my housing dashboard
  2. Open a new student app and pay a fee of $200 for reservations
  3. Complete and submit application

Emergency Housing

There are multiple situations where a person could need emergency housing. There are programs that can help people in abusive situations, people who are in need of temporary housing and programs that assist people who are being evicted. The link below has different resources organized by the type of emergency housing situation. If you find yourself in need of emergency housing this website is a great resource. Explore our emergency housing resources for more information.

Eligibility Requirements

Eligibility for low income housing is available for US Citizens and through a special program for non-citizens. Eligibility is based on location and a HACM application, which will be reviewed based on family composition, assets, and income.

Applications are available for Salinas locations, and waitlist applications are available for select properties in Monterey County. Section 8 housing Applications
